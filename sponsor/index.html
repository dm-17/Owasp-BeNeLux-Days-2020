Sponsoship packages

There are 3 combined sponsorship packages (Gold, Silver or Bronze) that cover the BeNeLux chapter meetings 2020 and the BeNeLux OWASP Days 2020.

Download our sponsor brochure (tbd) and contact us seba@owasp.org for questions or sponsorship confirmation!

Your sponsorship will be invested directly in the chapter meetings, supporting speaker and catering expenses.

The sponsorship will also be dedicated to cover the costs of the OWASP 2020 BeNeLux event.
